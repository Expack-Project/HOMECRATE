 About our Brand

 Vision

At Homecrate, we envision a world where distance is no barrier to warmth and belonging, enabling expats to effortlessly embrace their cultural roots and cherish the nostalgic memories that make them feel truly at home.

 Values

  • Connection: Building bridges across cultures and distances, fostering meaningful connections between expats and their homelands.
  • Inclusivity: Embracing diversity and creating a welcoming environment for expats from all backgrounds, celebrating each unique story.
  • Nostalgia: Thoughtfully curating packages that evoke cherished memories and ignite the senses, encapsulating the essence of home in every delivery.
