 About our Brand

 Vision

At Homecrate, we envision a world where distance is no barrier to warmth and belonging, empowering expats to effortlessly embrace their cultural roots and cherish the memories that make them feel truly connected to home, no matter where they are.

 Values

  • Deep bonds with ones roots: At Homecrate, we believe in going beyond physical distances to maintain hconnections between expats and their homelands. Our dedication to deepening these bonds sets us apart and allows us to create a unique, lasting impact on the lives of our customers.
  • Global Empathy: We strive to create an understanding community that transcends borders. By celebrating the unique stories of expats worldwide, Homecrate cultivates a relatable and inclusive environment that values the richness of cultural experiences.
  • Timeless Comfort: Homecrate's unwavering commitment to evoking cherished memories and delivering authentic, comforting experiences lies at the heart of our brand. We thoughtfully curate packages that resonate with expats on a personal level, bringing hope, inspiration, and a feeling of belonging that transcends time and place.
