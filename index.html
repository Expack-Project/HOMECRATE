At Homecrate, we strive to help expats connect and reconent with their roots, bringing their culture to them when they cannot come to it!

We are excited to share the Homecrate experience with you, and help you feel closer to home, no matter where you are. Stay tuned for more exciting updates in future!

 Who are we?

At Homecrate, we bridge the gap between home and heart for expats across the globe. We all know that living in a foreign country can be exciting, but we also understand the longing for the familiar comforts of home. That's why we've made it our mission to deliver specially curated packages filled with food, goodies, and nostalgic items from YOUR homeland, carefully selected to envoke cherished memories and satisfy your cravings!

Our professional team is dedicated to creating a sense of warmth and belonging - because we believe that even when you're miles away, you should never be too far from what you love about your home. Embrace your roots and celebrate your unique heritage with Homecrate - your personal connection to the tastes, scents, and sentiments of home!

 The Product!

Every "Homecrate" is a specially curated package for expats living abroad. These packages contain items, foodstuffs and goodies from the expats home country, and cannot be gotten locally, in current location.

Our tailor-made packages are crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail, offering a unique blend of local items that evoke cherished memories and celebrate your cultural roots.

Each Homecrate package is customized based on your home country, ensuring an authentic and personal experience that resonates deeply with your heritage. Our expert team handpicks a delightful assortment of items, including:

  • Traditional Snacks and Treats: Savor the flavors of your homeland with a selection of delicious snacks, sweets, and local delicacies that instantly transport your taste buds back to familiar times and places.
  • Artisanal Goods: Discover handcrafted treasures made by local artisans from your home country, showcasing the craftsmanship and heritage that make your culture unique.
  • Cultural Keepsakes: Unearth timeless mementos that capture the essence of your culture's customs and traditions, from festive decorations to symbolic trinkets and more.
  • Nostalgic Surprises: Delight in unexpected surprises that evoke a sense of nostalgia and connection to your roots, carefully chosen to spark joy and fond memories.

Whether you're longing for a taste of home, looking for the perfect gift, or seeking to support relocating employees, Homecrate is here to bridge the gap between home and heart, providing an unmatched experience that transcends distance and embraces the beauty of cultural diversity.

 Meet the team!

Meet the trio behind Homecrate: Samuel, Sanne, and Mauro. United by a common vision, these three students set out to make a difference in the lives of expats across the globe. Samuel, an expat from Slovakia, understands firsthand the emotions and challenges that come with living abroad. Alongside Sanne and Mauro, both hailing from the Netherlands where Homecrate is based, the team recognized a problem faced by countless individuals - the yearning for a taste of home and the comfort it brings.

Drawing from their diverse backgrounds and shared experiences, Samuel, Sanne, and Mauro are committed to solving this problem by bringing the essence of home to expats everywhere. They combine their individual talents and expertise to create personalized packages that evoke cherished memories and connect people with their roots.

 Inclusion

Our mission is to create an inclusive environment where people from all corners of the world can come together to share their unique stories, experiences, and perspectives.

As we continue to evolve and grow, we remain committed to our core values of empathy, respect, and cultural exchange. We believe that by promoting a deeper understanding of the complexities of expat life, we can help build bridges across cultures and foster greater appreciation and respect for our differences.

At Homecrate, we believe that diversity is our greatest strength. We celebrate the richness of cultural experiences that make our world so vibrant and unique. By promoting cross-cultural understanding and acceptance, we hope to create a more compassionate and inclusive world for all.

 Timeless Comfort

The value proposition being expressed here is that Homecrate is dedicated to providing its customers with an experience that evokes feelings of comfort, nostalgia, and a sense of belonging, no matter where they are in the world. Homecrate understands that expats may feel disconnected from their home country and culture, and they seek to bridge that gap by thoughtfully curating packages that cater to the unique needs and desires of their customers.

By using the value "Timeless Comfort," Homecrate emphasizes the lasting emotional impact of their products and services. They want their customers to feel a sense of familiarity and warmth when they receive their packages, as if they were transported back to a familiar place and time.

Overall, Homecrate's value proposition revolves around the idea of providing a personalized, authentic, and emotionally resonant experience that transcends physical boundaries and creates a sense of home wherever their customers may be.
