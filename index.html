 Who are we?

Welcome to Homecrate, where we bridge the gap between home and heart for expats across the globe. We understand that living in a foreign land can be an exhilarating journey, but we also know the longing for the familiar comforts of home. That's why we've made it our mission to deliver specially curated packages filled with food, goodies, and nostalgic items from your homeland, carefully selected to ignite your cherished memories and satisfy your cravings.

Our professional team is dedicated to creating a sense of warmth and belonging, because we believe that even when you're miles away, you should never be too far from the essence of home. Embrace your roots and celebrate your unique heritage with Homecrate - your personal connection to the tastes, scents, and sentiments that make you feel truly at home.

The Product!

Introducing Homecrate: A lovingly curated service designed to bring the essence of home to expats across the globe. Our tailor-made packages are crafted with the utmost care and attention to detail, offering a unique blend of food, goodies, and nostalgic items that evoke cherished memories and celebrate your cultural roots.

Each Homecrate package is customized based on your home country, ensuring an authentic and personal experience that resonates deeply with your heritage. Our expert team handpicks a delightful assortment of items, including:

  • Traditional Snacks and Treats: Savor the flavors of your homeland with a selection of delicious snacks, sweets, and local delicacies that instantly transport your taste buds back to familiar times and places.
  • Artisanal Goods: Discover handcrafted treasures made by local artisans from your home country, showcasing the craftsmanship and heritage that make your culture unique.
  • Cultural Keepsakes: Unearth timeless mementos that capture the essence of your culture's customs and traditions, from festive decorations to symbolic trinkets and more.
  • Nostalgic Surprises: Delight in unexpected surprises that evoke a sense of nostalgia and connection to your roots, carefully chosen to spark joy and fond memories.

    • Whether you're an expat longing for a taste of home, a thoughtful friend looking for the perfect gift, or a global corporation seeking to support relocating employees, Homecrate is here to bridge the gap between home and heart, providing an unmatched experience that transcends distance and embraces the beauty of cultural diversity.

     Meet the team!

    Meet the passionate trio behind Homecrate: Samuel, Sanne, and Mauro. United by a common vision, these three visionaries set out to make a difference in the lives of expats across the globe. Samuel, an expat from Slovakia, understands firsthand the emotions and challenges that come with living abroad. Alongside Sanne and Mauro, both hailing from the Netherlands where Homecrate is based, the team recognized a problem faced by countless individuals - the yearning for a taste of home and the comfort it brings.

    Drawing from their diverse backgrounds and shared experiences, Samuel, Sanne, and Mauro are committed to solving this problem by bringing the essence of home to expats everywhere. They combine their individual talents and expertise to create personalized packages that evoke cherished memories and connect people with their roots. Their unwavering passion for community and inclusivity has driven Homecrate's mission, transforming it from a mere idea into a thriving service that touches lives and creates a sense of belonging.

    At Homecrate, our founders' commitment to celebrating the unique stories of expats worldwide is at the heart of everything we do. Samuel, Sanne, and Mauro continue to inspire the team as we work together to nurture connections, foster happiness, and help expats feel a little closer to home.
